Announcement Regarding Lawsuit Against Alliance Residential, LLC. For Labor Code Violations

Yoon Law, APC and Justice Law Corporation have filed a putative class action lawsuit against Alliance Residential, LLC (“Alliance”) in Sacramento County Superior Court.

This is not a lawsuit against you; you are not being sued.

The lawsuit alleges that Alliance illegally denied its on-site non-exempt employees in California all wages owed, including overtime wages, minimum wages, premium wages for working through rest and meal breaks and failed to reimburse for business related expenses. The case was filed on behalf of all current and former hourly-paid or non-exempt on-site employees who worked for Alliance in California any time since August 26, 2012.

WE NEED YOUR SIGNED AUTHORIZATION

We believe the company violates California law by not providing breaks, having employees work without being paid (for example, working while not “on the clock”), knowingly pressuring employees to skip meals and breaks, to incur unreimbursed expenses for the benefit of Alliance (like cell phone usage), and are looking for people willing to authorize us to get their records.

We would like to request time and payroll records from Alliance because this will assist us in proving our case, which will benefit everyone including you. To get your records, we need your written authorization.
